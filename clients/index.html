Our Story

A Sanctuary Where Luxury
Meets Serenity

Rendezvous Jungle Resort was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where modern luxury coexists harmoniously with the untouched beauty of Wayanad's forests. We invite you to experience an escape that rejuvenates mind, body, and spirit.

Our commitment extends beyond providing exceptional accommodation; it embraces environmental stewardship and a meticulous attention to every detail of your stay. We ensure every moment is crafted for your utmost comfort and delight.

Our dedicated staff delivers genuine warmth and personalized service, ensuring your time with us is not just a visit, but an unforgettable, restorative experience where you feel truly cared for and connected to nature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about your stay, our services, and resort policies at Rendezvous Jungle Resort.

Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.

To ensure the comfort of all our guests and protect the natural wildlife, pets are generally not permitted at Rendezvous Jungle Resort.

Our resort offers fine dining with farm-to-table Kerala cuisine. We are happy to accommodate vegetarian and vegan meal preferences; please inform us upon booking.

Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort. We strive to provide reliable speeds for your connectivity needs amidst the natural setting.
