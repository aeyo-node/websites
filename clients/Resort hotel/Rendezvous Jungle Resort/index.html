Reserve Your Tranquil Escape

A Sanctuary Where Nature Meets Unrivaled Luxury.

Rendezvous Jungle Resort was born from a vision: to create a sanctuary where the modern comforts of luxury coexist harmoniously with the untouched, vibrant beauty of Wayanad’s forests. We believe in providing an escape, a place where the hustle of life fades into the whispers of the jungle.

Our commitment extends beyond hospitality. It's an environmental stewardship, meticulous attention to every detail, and the genuine warmth of our staff that frames each stay as an unforgettable, restorative experience. Here, you don't just visit nature; you become part of its serene embrace.

Questions, Answered.

Everything you need to know for your perfect escape.

Check-in is at 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

To ensure a serene experience for all our guests, pets are generally not allowed on the property. Please contact us directly for any specific assistance animals.

Our fine dining restaurant offers a variety of farm-to-table cuisine showcasing Kerala's flavors. We are pleased to accommodate vegetarian and vegan dietary preferences. Please inform us of any specific requirements when booking.

Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the resort. While we aim for reliable connectivity, please note that internet speeds can vary due to our secluded jungle location. It's best for essential communication rather than heavy streaming.

While our focus is on tranquil adult retreats, families are welcome. We can suggest gentle nature walks and local attractions suitable for children. Please contact our concierge for personalized recommendations.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the booking type and season. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions provided at the time of your reservation, or contact us for details.
