About The Firm

Modern Accounting.

Ashwin C Nair & Co. is a leading Chartered Accountancy firm providing premium professional services in corporate taxation, modern financial auditing, tax compliance, and commercial structural planning.

Strategically headquartered near the AKG Centre in Thiruvananthapuram, our practice integrates deep regulatory technical expertise with an empathetic, client-first consulting perspective. We guide businesses and high-net-worth individuals through ever-evolving legislative frameworks with clarity.

Whether structuring a new startup liability model, planning multi-state GST distributions, or assuring external compliance frameworks, our clients rely on our rigorous precision and complete data confidentiality.
